Frequently Asked Questions

What is Netflix?

+

Netflix is a streaming service that offers a wide variety of movies, TV shows, documentaries, and more. You can watch it on your computer, TV, smartphone, tablet, or game console.

How much does Netflix cost?

+

The price of Netflix varies depending on the plan you choose. There are three plans to choose from: Basic, Standard, and Premium. You can find more information about pricing on the Netflix website.

Where can I watch?

+

You can watch Netflix on your computer, TV, smartphone, tablet, or game console. You can also watch it on some smart TVs and Blu-ray players.

How do I cancel?

+

You can cancel your Netflix subscription at any time. To cancel, simply log in to your account and follow the instructions on the website.

What can I watch on Netflix?

+

Netflix offers a wide variety of movies, TV shows, documentaries, and more. You can find something for everyone on Netflix, from classic films to the latest blockbusters.

Is Netflix good for kids?

+

Netflix has a large selection of kids' movies and TV shows. You can find content for kids of all ages, from toddlers to teenagers. You can also use parental controls to limit the type of content your kids can watch.
